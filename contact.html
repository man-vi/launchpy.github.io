Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will I hear back after contacting you?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend giving us a call directly.

Can I schedule a call with an advisor?

Yes! You can schedule a 30-minute consultation with one of our program advisors to discuss your goals and determine if our AI Engineering program is right for you. Please email us at admissions@launchpy.com or use our contact form to request a call.

Do you offer information sessions?

Yes, we host virtual information sessions every two weeks where you can learn more about our program, meet instructors, and ask questions. Check our events calendar or contact us to register for the next available session.
